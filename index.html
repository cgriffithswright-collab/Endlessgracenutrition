Our Story

We are not coaches
who read about it.

Endless Grace Nutrition was started by Jackie and Cassandra, a mother and daughter who know exactly what it feels like to search for answers and come up empty.

Cassandra spent years trying to figure out her health. Different plans, different advice, nothing that actually fit her life as a working mother. It was not until her mother Jackie became a certified nutrition coach and built something specifically for her that things finally clicked.

That experience is the whole reason this business exists. Jackie found her passion in nutrition and brought it home first. Now they are bringing it to you.

We are not selling a generic plan. We are building something that fits your life the way nothing else has.

We serve people who are done with solutions that were never built with them in mind. Whether you are a busy parent, managing a health condition, feeding a whole family, or just ready to finally figure it out, we are here for that.

The Team

Two people. One goal.

A certified nutrition coach and an operations professional working together so our clients always get the best of both.

Certified ISSA Nutrition Coach
Jackie
Jackie handles all intake consultations, reviews and approves every plan before delivery, and leads all one-on-one video calls with clients. Her passion for nutrition started at home and grew into a calling. Jackie is currently completing her Obesity and Diabetes Management certification, expected spring 2026. This means even more specialized support is coming for clients managing those conditions.
Operations and Client Manager
Cassandra
Cassandra is your primary point of contact for everything day to day. She builds your personalized plan alongside Jackie, manages your onboarding, delivers your welcome kit, leads your weekly check-ins, and keeps you accountable from day one.
